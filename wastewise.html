The Problem

Restaurants run on guesswork.
The margins pay the price.

Every morning, your kitchen manager estimates how much to prep. They look at yesterday's numbers, factor in nothing about the weather or a nearby cricket match, and then cook accordingly.

The result? Overproduction on slow days. Shortfalls on busy ones. Produce spoils, margin bleeds, and no one has hard data to make it better tomorrow.

For multi-outlet operations, this problem compounds at every location — silently eroding profitability with no visibility into where the waste is coming from.

The Solution

Precision prep planning, powered by AI.

WasteWise replaces guesswork with data-driven prep recommendations tailored to your menu, your outlet, and today's demand signals — every single day.

It's not a generic inventory tool. It's a daily operations layer that sits between your POS data and your kitchen team, turning raw transaction history into actionable prep targets and real-time spoilage recovery.

The result: less food cooked unnecessarily, less food thrown away, and a new revenue stream from near-expiry discounting you were previously leaving on the table.
